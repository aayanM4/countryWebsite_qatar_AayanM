Qatar Landmarks

Souq Waqif

a marketplace in Doha, the capital of Qatar.

Barzan Towers

historic watchtowers in Umm Salal Mohammed, Qatar

Doha Corniche

a stunning 7-kilometer waterfront promenade in Doha, Qatar

The Pearl Island

a famous man-made island off the coast of Doha, Qatar

Doha Fort/Al Koot Fort

a historic 19th-century military fortress in the heart of Doha, Qatar, built in the 1880s to protect the nearby Souq Waqif traders from thieves

Aspire Tower

a distinctive 300-meter skyscraper in Doha, Qatar's Aspire Zone

National Museum of Qatar

an immersive museum in Doha, Qatar, located at the south end of the Corniche

Museum of Islamic Art

a world-renowned cultural landmark in Doha, Qatar

Hamad bin Khalifa Stadium

multi-purpose stadium in Doha, Qatar

Mathaf: Arab Museum of Modern Art

a unique museum in Doha, Qatar, dedicated to modern and contemporary art from the Arab world, Qatar

Al Wajbah Fort

a historic Qatari landmark near Al Rayyan

Villaggio Mall

a large, Venetian-themed shopping and entertainment complex in Doha, Qatar

Ahmad bin Ali Stadium

a modern, multi-purpose football stadium in Al Rayyan, Qatar

Katara Cultural Village

a major cultural hub in Doha, Qatar, designed to blend traditional Qatari heritage with modern architecture and arts

Lusail Stadium in Lusail

the largest stadium in Qatar, that was built for the 2022 FIFA World Cup
